About Us

Welcome to Paw Inn, where your pets are our family! Located in the Georgetown, our pet hotel offers a cozy and safe haven for your beloved furry friends. Founded by passionate pet lovers, we provide top-notch boarding, daycare, and grooming services in a state-of-the-art facility designed with your pet's comfort in mind. Our dedicated team ensures personalized care and 24/7 supervision, giving you peace of mind while you're away. Choose Paw Inn for a home-away-from-home experience your pets will love!

Dog Boarding Policy

  1. General Requirement

    • Vaccinations: All dogs must be up-to-date on vaccinations including rabies, distemper, parvovirus, and Bordetella (kennel cough). Proof of vaccinations is required.

    • Health: Dogs must be free from contagious diseases and parasites. A health check and flea/tick prevention are required prior to boarding.

    • Behavior: Dogs must be non-aggressive and socialized. Any history of aggression must be disclosed.


  2. Reservations

    • Reservations: Must be made in advance. A deposit may be required to secure a spot.


  3. Check in and Check out

    • Check-In: Available between 10:00 AM and 6:00 PM. Late check-ins may incur an additional fee.

    • Check-Out: By 6:00 PM on the day of departure. Late check-outs may incur an additional fee.


  4. Feeding and Medication

    • Special dietary: Owners must provide enough food for the duration of the stay.

    • Medication: All medications must be provided with clear instructions. An additional fee may be charged for administering medications.


  5. Personal Items

    • Bedding/Toys: Owners are encouraged to bring familiar bedding and toys. However, the facility is not responsible for lost or damaged items.


  6. Emergency Care

    • Veterinary Care: In case of illness or injury, the facility will seek veterinary care at the owner's expense. Owners must provide emergency contact information.

    • Emergency Contact: Owners must provide at least one emergency contact who is available during the boarding period.


  7. Behavior and Safety

    • Aggressive Behavior: Dogs displaying aggressive behavior may be separated from other dogs or sent home.

What To Pack

Bring Your Own Food

Use small plastic baggies to package pre-measured meals and label them.

Dietary Supplements

Indicate the appropriate number of doses that your dog will need to take in your absence.

Documents Required

Be sure that all required documents and forms are ready to present on drop off day.

A reminder of Home

To keep your dog feeling close to you in your absence, include something with your scent.
